London has a wide variety of attractions and activities to see and do. As a cultural hub in both the U.K. and Europe, London attracts people of all creeds and colours. These people bring both unique perspectives and cultural differences. This means that there are many one-of-a-kind attractions in London. From Trapeze school and God's own Junkyard to 'Dans le Noir', a restaurant in complete darkness and staffed by blind waiters and waitresses.
