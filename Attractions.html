London has a wide variety of attractions and activities to see and do. As a cultural hub in both the U.K. and Europe, London attracts people of all creeds and colours. These people bring both unique perspectives and cultural differences. This means that there are many one-of-a-kind attractions in London. From Trapeze school and God's own Junkyard to 'Dans le Noir', a restaurant in complete darkness.

God's Own Junkyard

A gallery with a difference, God's Own Junkyard is a large collection of new, used and reclaimed neon signs. Perfect for a photoshoot with a twist.

Dans Le Noir

An interesting take on fine dining, this restaurant is in complete darkness. The food becomes all the tastier when your other senses are limited. You are also served exclusively by blind waiters and waitresses.

Dans Le Noir
69-73 St John Street
London
